Intro

I am an Embedded Systems Engineer focused on bridging the gap between hardware and software. With a comprehensive background in the full product development lifecycle, I handle everything from schematic design and PCB artwork to component sourcing and SMT production management. Beyond hardware, I specialize in firmware optimization and building stable control systems. I enjoy the challenge of debugging complex logic and ensuring data integrity in real-time environments, delivering robust, production-ready solutions. Recently, I have been deeply interested in AI hardware accelerators and FPGA-based design, aiming to push the boundaries of high-performance edge computing.

Key Features: Designed a Built-In Self-Test (BIST) JIG for automated hardware verification. Integrated power monitoring logic (PAC195x) to measure real-time voltage and current. Implemented diagnostic firmware to detect hardware failures automatically.

4. LED Driver

Key Features: Developed a constant current LED driver circuit with PWM-based dimming control. Optimized firmware to ensure flicker-free operation and efficient power management. Conducted thermal testing and reliability verification.
Tech Stack: AVR (ATmega), Cortex-M4 (STM32), Parallel Interface.
